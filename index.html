About me

Hello, I'm Kaylee Van Aalst. I am currently a student at Jönköping University, pursuing a degree in New Media Design. With a passion for creativity and technology, I am honing my skills in areas such as user experience (UX) design, digital media, and interactive technologies. Jönköping University provides a dynamic and collaborative environment, where I have the opportunity to work on both theoretical and practical aspects of design, while constantly engaging with the latest trends in new media. Throughout my studies, I have developed a keen understanding of how design shapes user interactions and experiences, and I’m always excited to tackle challenges that blend aesthetics with functionality. My education at Jönköping University has given me a solid foundation in design thinking, and I have gained hands-on experience with various design tools, coding languages, and prototyping methods. As I continue to learn and grow, I am eager to apply my knowledge in real-world projects, explore new media technologies, and contribute to innovative solutions that enhance the way people engage with digital content. I am excited about the potential to shape the future of media design and communication through my creative and technical skills.
